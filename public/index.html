About Us

Founded in 2007, **Section 8 Bass** is a Denver-based record label that dives deep into the dark and militant realms of bass music. Specializing in Drum and Bass, Dubstep, and other heavy bass genres, Section 8 Bass has become a hub for innovative and boundary-pushing artists.

Our roster includes some of the most prominent names in the bass music scene, such as Sinister Souls, Sick Cycle, emmeeji, KOROstyle, Von D, Artroniks, Megalodon, TRUTH, Current Value, Dodge & Fuski, and SPL.

Our Music

Section 8 Bass is known for its diverse and powerful discography. Notable releases include:

  • Biobird - Excess EP
  • Biobird - The Expanse
  • KOROstyle & Gutcha - Back in Briz
  • Sick Cycle - Deep In The Woods
